Questionnaires & Surveys

As part of this research project, I occasionally collect feedback and data through surveys. Your responses help me understand how people learn about politics and what kinds of civic experiences matter most. Participation is completely voluntary and responses are anonymous unless otherwise noted.

Current Surveys

At the moment, there are no active questionnaires. When new surveys are available, links will appear here. If you’re interested in participating, please check back later or contact me.

Proposed Questionnaires

In the near future, I plan to launch surveys on topics such as:

  • Youth perceptions of democracy and political efficacy
  • Effects of civic simulation programs on student attitudes
  • Barriers to participation in local politics

If you have suggestions for questions or would like to be notified when questionnaires are live, feel free to reach out via the contact information on the home page.
