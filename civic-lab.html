Civic Lab

Campus Civic Blueprint Services

The Civic Lab is a values‑aligned consulting and resource hub that helps student governments, civic clubs and small colleges create vibrant, student‑centered democracies. Drawing on years of experience running the Maryland Student Legislature chapter and designing democratic curricula, Nathan Sanchez offers practical tools and personalized guidance to help your organization thrive.

Starter Civic Blueprint Package

Ideal for new student governments or organizations looking to get off the ground. This package includes:

  • A customized constitution and bylaws template tailored to your campus or club’s needs.
  • Sample agendas, recruitment scripts and communications to kick‑start engagement.
  • One 60‑minute virtual consultation to review your structure and answer questions.
  • A follow‑up implementation guide with tips on elections, budgeting and member training.

Pricing: Please contact us for a quote based on your organization’s size and goals.

Custom Consulting & Workshops

For established organizations or institutions seeking deeper support, the Civic Lab offers tailored services such as:

  • Facilitated workshops on democratic skills (agenda setting, deliberation, consensus building and parliamentary procedure).
  • One‑on‑one advising for leaders on strategic planning and engagement initiatives.
  • Curriculum co‑design for hands‑on democracy programs like mock legislatures or civic labs.

Pricing: Custom packages are designed to fit your budget. Inquire for details.

FAQ

Is this partisan? No. The Civic Lab focuses on developing democratic skills and structures rather than advocating for any particular party or ideology.

Who is this for? Student governments, issue‑based clubs, departments and smaller institutions looking to build or reinvigorate their civic programs.

Why does it cost money? Providing high‑quality consulting and resources takes time and expertise. Your support helps Nathan continue his studies and expand democratic programming.
