Civic Engagement Experiment & Lab

Exploring how people learn democracy.

Part personal site, part research notebook, part experimental playground.

This site is a living lab for understanding civic engagement. Here you’ll find polished journal memos, youth-oriented civic explainers, interactive prototypes, and notes on everything from democratic theory to campaign field work. It’s also the master hub tying together my campus experiment, Maryland Student Legislature work, and long-term research on democratic erosion and civic education.

Campus experiment: democratic engagement at St. Mary’s College of Maryland.
Scope: essays, data, field notes, and public-facing explainers.

What this project is tracking

  • Democratic erosion & inequality: connecting theory to youth attitudes and behavior.
  • Campus experiments: testing small-scale interventions in a real college environment.
  • Field work: campaigns, Maryland General Assembly exposure, and organizational leadership.
  • Media & culture: how music, pop culture, and narratives shift civic imagination.
