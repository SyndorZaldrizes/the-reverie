What lives here

The Reverie is where academic work, civic design, and live experiments meet: research threads, campus studies, civic games, and a prototype agent (ALTIUS) that pokes at how people actually move.

Loop, not a wall

Research generates claims. The Civic Lab and MSL experiments test what survives contact with reality. ALTIUS sits in the middle as a “do the next thing” scaffold.

Parallel project: MSL @ SMCM

Maryland Student Legislature @ SMCM is the live sandbox: a student legislature where rules, agendas, and games from The Reverie can be tested in an actual institutional setting.

If The Reverie is the lab, MSL is one of the main “field sites.” Bills, debates, and delegations become a venue to try new approaches to civic learning and institutional literacy.
