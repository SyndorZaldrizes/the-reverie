How this works

This is a public-facing shell of ALTIUS inside The Reverie. Its job is to convert stuck intention into concrete action. It will not do your work for you.

How to use it:

  • Describe a real task, problem, or civic dilemma youâ€™re avoiding.
  • ALTIUS identifies the real barrier and gives the smallest next step.
  • Your job: do the step, then come back.
Important: ALTIUS is built around agency, not dependency. If you try to hand it your life, it will hand it back in smaller, actionable pieces.

Best prompts to try:

  • "I have a project due in 10 days and I'm frozen."
  • "I want to get involved in my community but feel useless."
  • "I care about politics but everything feels rigged. What can I do?"
