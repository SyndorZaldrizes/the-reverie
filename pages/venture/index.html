What this venture is

The Venture track is the “build something real” sibling of the research program. Research produces claims; Venture produces tools, workflows, and services that can survive real constraints.

One-line mission: Make civic participation feel doable again by reducing friction, clarifying choices, and creating nonpartisan pathways from awareness → action.

The problem

Many people want to engage but bounce off the same walls: confusion about processes, social intimidation, polarization fatigue, and a constant sense that “it won’t matter.” The venture is built to attack those walls with practical systems, not speeches.

Core offerings (WIP)

These are intentionally modular so you can pilot them on campus before scaling.
